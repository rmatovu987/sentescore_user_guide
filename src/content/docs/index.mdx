## How SenteScore Works

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="1 — Define Scoring Parameters" icon="pencil">
    Create custom field definitions from any data source — income, repayment history, employment stability, debt-to-income ratios, and alternative data signals. Set weights, thresholds, and scoring bands that reflect your lending philosophy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2 — Build Your Approval Workflow" icon="random">
    Map the full loan lifecycle — from application submission and automated scoring, through manual review levels, approval, and disbursement — with conditional routing, escalation paths, and configurable checklists at every step.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3 — Process Applications at Scale" icon="rocket">
    Loan officers submit applications from a single hub. Every application is automatically scored in real time, routed through your configured workflow, and tracked with a complete field-level audit trail from submission to final decision.
  </Card>
  <Card title="4 — Audit, Export & Analyse" icon="information">
    Every scoring decision is explainable, logged, and traceable. Export any list to PDF, Excel, CSV, or clipboard. The system-wide audit log captures every change across applications, users, roles, and settings — append-only and tamper-proof.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
