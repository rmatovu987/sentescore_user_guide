The **Roles** page allows administrators to define and manage the roles within their organisation. Roles are central to how SenteScore controls access and routes workflow approvals — every user is assigned a role, and every workflow level is mapped to a role that determines who can review and action applications at that stage.

Navigate here via **Home → Settings → Business Settings → Roles**.

---

![Roles page](/images/settings-roles.png)

## Page Overview

The page lists all configured roles in the organisation. A **New Role** button in the top-right allows administrators to create additional roles as the team grows.

---

## Table Columns

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **ID** | The unique alphanumeric identifier for the role. Click it to open the role detail and edit it. |
| **Name** | The display name of the role (e.g. *Credit Officer*, *Finance Officer*). This name is used when assigning roles to workflow levels and to users. |
| **Description** | A short explanation of the role's purpose within the organisation. |
| **Status** | Whether the role is currently **Active** or inactive. Only active roles can be assigned to users and workflow levels. |

---

## Default Roles

SenteScore comes pre-configured with the following roles out of the box:

| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| **Super Admin** | Super Administrator with full platform access and control. |
| **Branch Manager** | Manages branch operations. |
| **Credit Officer** | Evaluates creditworthiness of applicants. |
| **MSME Officer** | Handles MSME arbitration within the approval chain. |
| **Finance Officer** | Processes disbursements at the final workflow level. |
| **Loan Officer** | Initiates loan applications on behalf of applicants. |

<Aside type="note">
  These roles map directly to the levels in your configured approval workflows. For example, a workflow with three levels might assign Credit Officer → MSME Officer → Finance Officer at Levels 1, 2, and 3 respectively.
</Aside>

---

## Role Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | The role is live and can be assigned to users and workflow levels. |
| **Inactive** | The role has been disabled. It cannot be assigned to new users or workflow levels. |

<Aside type="caution">
  Deactivating a role does not automatically remove it from users or workflow levels it has already been assigned to. Review existing assignments before deactivating a role to avoid disruption to the approval chain.
</Aside>

---

## Search & Export

Use the **search bar** to filter roles by name, description, or ID. The **Export** button supports the following formats:

- **PDF** — for sharing with stakeholders or compliance records
- **Excel** — `.xlsx` for record-keeping or analysis
- **CSV** — plain-text for importing into other tools
- **Copy to Clipboard** — for quickly pasting into another application

Use **Column Visibility** to show or hide specific columns in the table.
