Roles define the functional responsibilities of users in your organisation and control which parts of the platform they can access. Creating a role makes it available for assignment to users and to workflow approval levels.

Navigate to **Home → Settings → Business Settings → Roles** and click the **New Role** button in the top-right corner.

---

![New Role modal](/images/create-role.png)

## Form Fields

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Name** | ✅ Yes | The display name of the role (e.g. *Risk Analyst*, *Regional Manager*). This name appears in user assignments and workflow level configuration. |
| **Description** | No | A short explanation of the role's purpose (e.g. *Reviews high-value loan applications*). Helps administrators understand the role's intended function at a glance. |

---

## Creating the Role

<Steps>
1. Navigate to **Settings → Business Settings → Roles** and click **New Role**.
2. Enter a **Name** for the role.
3. Optionally add a **Description** to clarify the role's purpose.
4. Click **Create Role** to save.
</Steps>

The new role appears in the Roles list immediately with **Active** status. You can then assign it to users and configure its permissions from the [Role Detail](/guides/role-detail) page.

<Aside type="note">
  After creating a role, you will need to configure its **Permissions** (System, Workflow Management, Settings, Scoring) from the role detail page before assigning it to users.
</Aside>
