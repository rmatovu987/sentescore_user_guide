Administrators can edit a role to update its name or description at any time. Changes are reflected immediately across the platform and recorded in the role's [Timeline](/settings/business/roles/view-role#timeline).

To edit a role, navigate to **Settings → Business Settings → Roles**, click the role **ID** to open the [Role Detail](/settings/business/roles/view-role) page, then click **Edit**.

---

![Edit Role modal](/images/edit-role.png)

## Form Fields

The **Edit Role** modal opens pre-populated with the role's current values.

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Name** | ✅ Yes | The display name of the role. Updating this changes how the role appears in user assignments, workflow level configuration, and across the platform. |
| **Description** | No | A short explanation of the role's purpose. |

---

## Saving Changes

<Steps>
1. From the Role Detail page, click **Edit**.
2. Update the **Name** and/or **Description** as needed.
3. Click **Update Role** to save. Changes take effect immediately.
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
  Editing a role's name or description does not affect the permissions assigned to it or the users it is assigned to. To modify permissions, use the **Permissions** section on the [Role Detail](/guides/role-detail) page directly.
</Aside>
