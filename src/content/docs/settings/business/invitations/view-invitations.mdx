The **Invitations** page shows all users who have been invited to join your organisation on SenteScore. Invitations are the primary way to onboard new team members — an email is sent to the invitee with a link to set up their account.

Navigate here via **Home → Settings → Business Settings → Invitations**.

---

![Invitations page](/images/invitations.png)

## Page Overview

The table lists all sent invitations alongside the invitee's details and the current status of their invitation. A **Invite new user** button in the top-right opens the invitation form.

---

## Table Columns

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **ID** | The unique alphanumeric identifier for the invitation. Click it to open the [Invitation Detail](/settings/business/invitations/view-invitation) page. |
| **First Name** | The invitee's first name as entered during the invitation. |
| **Last Name** | The invitee's last name. |
| **Email** | The email address the invitation was sent to. |
| **Time Zone** | The time zone assigned to the invitee at the time of invitation. |
| **Status** | The current state of the invitation — see [Invitation Statuses](#invitation-statuses) below. |

---

## Invitation Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | The invitation has been sent but the invitee has not yet accepted it and set up their account. |
| **Accepted** | The invitee has clicked the invitation link, completed registration, and is now an active user. |
| **Expired** | The invitation link has expired before the invitee accepted it. A new invitation can be resent from the [Invitation Detail](/settings/business/invitations/view-invitation) page. |

<Aside type="tip">
  If an invitee has not accepted their invitation, you can resend it from the Invitation Detail page by clicking the invitation ID and using the **Resend Invite** button.
</Aside>

---

## Search & Export

Use the **search bar** to filter invitations by name, email, or ID. The **Export** button supports the following formats:

- **PDF** — for compliance records or sharing with stakeholders
- **Excel** — `.xlsx` for record-keeping or analysis
- **CSV** — plain-text for importing into other tools
- **Copy to Clipboard** — for quickly pasting into another application

Use **Column Visibility** to show or hide specific columns in the table.
