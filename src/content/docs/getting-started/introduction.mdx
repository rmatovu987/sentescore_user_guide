# Welcome to SenteScore

**SenteScore** is an AI-powered credit decisioning platform that gives lenders the tools to define dynamic credit parameters, build automated decision workflows, and manage every loan application — all in one intelligent platform.

Whether you're a microfinance institution processing hundreds of small-ticket loans a month or a large bank running multiple simultaneous lending products, SenteScore scales to fit your needs.

## Why SenteScore?

Traditional credit scoring is rigid, slow, and often excludes the very borrowers lenders want to reach. SenteScore changes that.
